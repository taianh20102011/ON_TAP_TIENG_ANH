Bị động Causative If Relative Clause It's time Not only…but also Từ nối
1
Câu bị động
Passive Voice
▼
S + be + V₃ (+ by O)
Chia "be" theo thì
Hiện tại đơn: am/is/are + V₃
Hiện tại tiếp diễn: am/is/are + being + V₃
Hiện tại hoàn thành: have/has + been + V₃
Quá khứ đơn: was/were + V₃
Quá khứ tiếp diễn: was/were + being + V₃
Tương lai đơn: will + be + V₃
→ The letter is written by him.
→ The cake was being eaten when I arrived.
→ The project has been completed already.
S + modal + be + V₃
→ This problem can be solved easily.
→ The report must be submitted today.
→ Mistakes should be avoided.
→ The door might be locked.
Modal + be + V₃ → không chia "be" theo chủ ngữ.
2
Thể nhờ vả
Causative
▼
Have – sắp xếp ai làm
have + O người + V (bare) have + O vật + V₃
→ I had him fix my car. (nhờ anh ấy sửa)
→ I had my car fixed. (xe được sửa)
Get – thuyết phục / nhờ
get + O người + to-V get + O vật + V₃
→ She got him to clean the room.
→ She got her hair cut.
Make – ép buộc (bị động: be made to-V)
make + O + V (bare)
→ The teacher made students rewrite the essay.
→ Students were made to rewrite it.
Let – cho phép (bị động: be allowed to-V)
let + O + V (bare)
→ Dad let me use the car.
Help – giúp đỡ
help + O + (to) V
→ She helped him (to) understand the problem.
3
Câu điều kiện (If)
Conditionals
▼
Loại 0 – Sự thật
If + HTĐ, HTĐ
→ If you heat water, it boils.
Loại 1 – Có thể xảy ra
If + HTĐ, will + V
→ If it rains, we will stay home.
Loại 2 – Không có thật (HT)
If + V₂/were, would + V
→ If I were rich, I would travel the world.
Loại 3 – Không có thật (QK)
If + had + V₃, would have + V₃
→ If she had studied, she would have passed.
Mixed – QK → HT
If + had V₃, would + V (HT)
→ If she had studied medicine, she would be a doctor now.
Mixed – HT → QK
If + V₂ (HT), would have + V₃
→ If I were braver, I would have spoken up.
Đảo ngữ (bỏ "If")
L1: Should you need help, call me.
L2: Were I you, I would apologize.
L3: Had I known, I would have told you.
Unless = if…not:
  Unless you hurry, you'll be late.

Provided that:
  Provided that you pass, you'll get the job.

As long as:
  You can stay as long as you're quiet.

Only if + đảo ngữ:
  Only if you study hard will you succeed.
4
Mệnh đề quan hệ
Relative Clause
▼
N + who / whom / which / that / whose / where / when + ...
who – người (chủ ngữ): The man who lives next door.
whom – người (tân ngữ): The girl whom I met.
which – vật: The book which I bought.
that – người hoặc vật (defining).
whose – sở hữu: The girl whose hair is long.
where – nơi  |  when – thời gian.
V-ing (chủ động)
→ The man standing there = who is standing there.
V₃ (bị động)
→ The letter written by him = which was written.
to-V (sau first / last / only / số thứ tự)
→ He is the first man to walk on the moon.
Non-defining – dấu phẩy, không dùng "that"
→ My brother, who lives in Hanoi, is a doctor.
What = the thing(s) that
→ What she said surprised everyone.
Which chỉ cả mệnh đề
→ He passed, which surprised everyone.
5
It's time – Wish – Would rather
Subjunctive
▼
Dạng 1 – nói chung
It's time + to-V
→ It's time to go.
Dạng 2 – đã đến lúc (nhẹ trách cứ)
It's (high/about) time + S + V₂
→ It's time you went to bed.
→ It's high time he started studying.
Ước hiện tại – trái thực tế
S + wish + S + V₂ / were
→ I wish I were taller.
Ước quá khứ – hối tiếc
S + wish + S + had + V₃
→ I wish I had studied harder.
Ước tương lai – muốn ai thay đổi
S + wish + S + would + V
→ I wish you would stop talking so loud.
Would rather
S + would rather + V (bare) [chính mình] S + would rather + S + V₂ [người khác]
→ I would rather stay home than go out.
→ I would rather you didn't tell him.
As if / As though
as if + S + V₂ → HT giả định as if + S + had V₃ → QK giả định
→ He talks as if he knew everything.
→ She cried as if she had lost everything.
6
Không những… mà còn
Not only…but also
▼
S + not only + A + but also + B Not only + aux + S + V, but S + also + V
→ She is not only smart but also kind.
→ Not only did he sing, but he also danced.
Khi "Not only" đứng đầu câu → đảo ngữ mệnh đề đó.
Both… and: She is both kind and smart.
Either… or: Take either the bus or the train.
Neither… nor: Neither he nor she was there.

No sooner… than:
  No sooner had she left than it rained.

Hardly… when:
  Hardly had I arrived when he called.
Trạng từ phủ định + aux + S + V
→ Never have I seen such beauty.
→ Seldom does she complain.
→ Little did he know the truth.
→ Only then did I understand.
→ Only if you try will you succeed.
→ So tired was she that she fell asleep.
7
Từ nối (Connectors)
Linking Words
▼
Thêm ý (Addition)
and, moreover, furthermore, in addition, besides, also, what is more
Tương phản (Contrast)
but, however, yet, nevertheless, whereas, while, on the other hand
Although / Even though + S + V, S + V Despite / In spite of + N / V-ing
→ Although it was expensive, she bought it.
→ Despite the rain, they played.
→ Much as I'd like to help, I can't.
→ No matter what you say, I won't change.
Không viết "Although… but…" — lỗi rất phổ biến!
Kết quả
so, therefore, thus, as a result, consequently, hence
Mục đích
to, in order to, so that, so as to, in order that, for fear that, lest
So… that / Such… that
So + adj/adv + that + S + V Such + (a/an) + adj + N + that + S + V
→ She was so tired that she fell asleep.
→ It was such a hard exam that many failed.
1
Bị động nâng cao
Passive Advanced
▼
See, hear, feel, watch…
S + be seen/heard + to-V (hoàn thành) S + be seen/heard + V-ing (tiếp diễn)
→ He was seen to enter the building.
→ She was heard singing in the shower.
Make / Let → bị động
→ He was made to work. (make → be made to-V)
→ We were allowed to go. (let → be allowed to-V)
It + be + V₃ + that-clause S + be + V₃ + to-V S + be + V₃ + to have + V₃
→ It is believed that he is guilty.
→ He is believed to be guilty.
→ He is believed to have committed the crime.
Verbs: believe, say, think, report, consider, know, suppose…
Động từ 2 tân ngữ → 2 câu bị động
Active: She gave him a book.
Passive 1: He was given a book.
Passive 2: A book was given to him.
Bị động với V-ing
enjoy / avoid / consider + being + V₃
→ She enjoys being praised.
→ He hates being ignored.
3
If – Đảo ngữ & Mixed nâng cao
Conditionals Adv.
▼
Đảo ngữ 3 loại
Should + S + V → Loại 1 Were + S + (to-V/adj) → Loại 2 Had + S + V₃ → Loại 3
→ Should you need help, call me.
→ Were she to arrive late, we'd start without her.
→ Had I known, I would have told you.
Mixed conditional
→ If I had taken the job, I would be rich now. (QK→HT)
→ If I were smarter, I would have solved it. (HT→QK)
Mixed = kết hợp 2 thì khác nhau trong 1 câu điều kiện.
6
Đảo ngữ nhấn mạnh toàn diện
Full Inversion
▼
Trạng từ/cụm phủ định + aux + S + V
Never have I seen such beauty.
Seldom does she complain.
Rarely did he arrive on time.
Little did he know the truth.
No sooner had she left than it rained.
Hardly had I arrived when he called.
Not only did he fail, but he also quit.
Only then did I understand.
Only if you try hard will you succeed.
So tired was she that she fell asleep immediately.
4
Relative Clause nâng cao
Relative Adv.
▼
Giới từ + which / whom (trang trọng)
N + prep + which/whom + S + V
→ The house in which she lives is old.
→ The man with whom I spoke is the director.
Which chỉ cả mệnh đề trước
→ He passed the exam, which surprised everyone.
Whose dùng cho cả vật
→ The company whose products are popular is expanding.
7
Từ nối nâng cao
Connectors Adv.
▼
Much as / Try as
→ Much as I'd like to help, I can't.
→ Try as he might, he couldn't open the door.
No matter + wh-
→ No matter what you say, I won't change my mind.
→ No matter how hard it is, don't give up.
→ No matter where she goes, she makes friends.
For fear that / Lest
S + V + for fear that + S + might/would + V S + V + lest + S + (should) + V
→ She whispered for fear that she might wake the baby.
→ He ran lest he should miss the train.
So that / In order that
→ He studied hard so that he could pass.
→ She spoke slowly in order that everyone could understand.
